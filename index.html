ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Music is so closely tied to culture and history, often it is an expression or response to such. We started wondering what other countries were listening to and researching the musical trends their experience. Through such, we discovered many interesting niche genres that evolve from region's musical histories. Music is a universal language, so we wanted to create a platform for people to discover and learn through it.


We hope you enjoy! :)
